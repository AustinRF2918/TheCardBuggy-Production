Humble Beginnings

Alex Fedrigo was fed up with having to pass the entire deck around when he played cards with his friends. It was clunky and inefficient. Being a life-long entrepreneur, he went to work on solving the problem. It took him long hours of design work, and working together with different teams to build it just right.

Summer of 2009, he finally came up with the perfect solution to playing cards with groups. He worked tirelessly on designing every part of the Card Buggy to make sure it was perfect for him and his friends. What he came up with is a great little tool sure to save you and your friends precious time playing cards!
